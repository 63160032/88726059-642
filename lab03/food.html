About Tom Kha Kai


Thai national dish

Tom Kha Kai is a famous Thai food that people around the world know. It is a Thai national dish that is so delicious that foreigners ask for it every time they come to Thailand. which is tom kha kai It is classified as a tom yum food. similar to tom yum goong strong flavor There is a mixture of herbs, but add coconut milk.This makes Tom Kha kai taste more mellow. less spicy and sour That's the reason why this Thai dish is very popular with foreigners. Although Tom Kha Kai has no history of beginning in any era. But this Thai dish has been in the Thai kitchen for a long time. popular to eat in the family Treat guests and make merit at auspicious events
